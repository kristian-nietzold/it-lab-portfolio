Shared Drives

Shared Drives configured for team-based ownership

Shared Drives were implemented to enforce organisational ownership of data, ensuring files remain accessible and controlled regardless of user lifecycle.

Benefits

  • Files remain accessible when users leave
  • Centralised management of team data
  • Improved collaboration within departments
Business justification:
Ensures critical business data is not tied to individual user accounts, reducing risk during offboarding or account removal.

Permission Management

Permissions managed using Google Groups

Access to Drive files and Shared Drives was controlled using Google Groups, enabling scalable and consistent permission management.

Approach

  • Permissions assigned to groups (not individuals)
  • Department-based access (HR, IT, Finance, etc.)
  • Consistent access control across resources
Why this matters:
Group-based permissions scale efficiently and minimise administrative overhead.

Security Impact

  • Prevents permission sprawl
  • Reduces risk of misconfigured access
  • Simplifies onboarding and offboarding
