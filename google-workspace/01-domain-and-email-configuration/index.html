Domain & Email Configuration

Domain verification, Gmail activation, mail routing, and email authentication setup.

Overview

This section demonstrates configuring a custom domain and securing email delivery within Google Workspace.

Domain Verification

Domain ownership was verified using a DNS CNAME record in Cloudflare.

Gmail Activation

Gmail was enabled for the domain to allow email sending and receiving.

MX Records

MX records were configured in Cloudflare to route email to Google servers.

SPF

SPF defines authorised mail servers.

v=spf1 include:_spf.google.com ~all

DKIM

DKIM signing was enabled to validate outgoing email authenticity.

DMARC

DMARC policy was added for monitoring and future enforcement.

Summary

  • Domain verified via DNS
  • Gmail enabled
  • MX routing configured
  • SPF, DKIM, DMARC implemented
