Domain & Email Configuration

Configured domain verification, Gmail activation, mail routing, and email authentication controls.

Overview

This section demonstrates the setup of a custom domain with Google Workspace, including DNS verification and secure email configuration.

Domain Verification

Domain ownership was verified using a DNS record provided by Google Workspace. A CNAME record was added in Cloudflare, and after propagation the domain was successfully verified in the Admin Console.

Gmail Activation

Gmail was enabled for the domain after verification, allowing users to send and receive email through Google Workspace.

MX Records (Mail Routing)

MX records were configured in Cloudflare to route inbound email to Google’s mail servers.

SPF (Sender Policy Framework)

An SPF record was added to define authorised sending servers.

v=spf1 include:_spf.google.com ~all

DKIM

DKIM keys were generated in Google Admin and added to Cloudflare DNS, enabling cryptographic signing of outbound email.

DMARC

A DMARC policy was configured to monitor and control authentication failures.

Summary

  • Verified domain ownership
  • Enabled Gmail
  • Configured MX routing
  • Implemented SPF, DKIM, and DMARC
