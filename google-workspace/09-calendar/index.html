Calendar & Resource Management

Organisational scheduling, meeting room booking, and hybrid collaboration setup.

Overview

Google Calendar was configured to support structured scheduling, resource booking, and seamless hybrid collaboration across the organisation.

Key concept:
Calendar is not just scheduling — it is a resource management and collaboration platform.

Resource Management (Meeting Rooms)

Resource management overview

Buildings and resources were configured to enable centralised management of meeting spaces across the organisation.

Capabilities

  • Define buildings and physical locations
  • Create bookable meeting rooms
  • Control resource visibility and usage
Real-world use:
Used by companies to manage meeting rooms, desks, and shared equipment.

Creating a Meeting Room Resource

Meeting room configuration

Configuration

  • Name: Meeting Room 1
  • Building: Office
  • Floor: 1
  • Capacity: 10 people
  • Description: Main office meeting room
Why this matters:
Enables accurate room booking, prevents overcrowding, and improves user experience.

Resource Structure & Availability

Resource list and hierarchy

  • Resources linked to buildings
  • Organised by location and type
  • Visible to users during scheduling
Benefit:
Makes it easy for users to find and book available rooms.

Booking a Meeting Room

Selecting a room during event creation

When creating a calendar event, users can search for and assign a meeting room.

What happens automatically

  • Room availability is checked
  • Room is reserved upon booking
  • Double booking is prevented
This eliminates scheduling conflicts across teams.

Hybrid Meetings (Meet + Room)

Calendar event with Meet + room

Meetings were configured with both physical rooms and Google Meet links.

Features

  • Automatic Google Meet link generation
  • Dial-in phone options
  • Support for remote participants
Real-world relevance:
This is standard in modern hybrid workplaces.

Design Approach

  • Centralised resource management

  • Integrated meeting + room booking
  • Hybrid collaboration support
This setup ensures efficient scheduling at scale.

Summary

  • Meeting rooms created and structured

  • Hybrid meetings fully supported
